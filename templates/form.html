Virginia Absentee Ballot Application

You can apply to vote absentee as early as one year before the election. Ballots are available 45 days before most elections. The deadline for receipt of your application to vote absentee by mail is 5:00 PM one week before the election. You can contact the State Board of Elections at 800-552-9745 or info@sbe.virginia.gov.
