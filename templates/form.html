Are you already registered to vote? If not, click here. If you are, continue below.

You can apply to vote absentee as early as one year before the election. Ballots are available 45 days before most elections. The deadline for receipt of your application to vote absentee by mail is 5:00 PM one week before the election. You can contact the Virginia Department of Elections at 804-864-8901 or info@elections.virginia.gov.
