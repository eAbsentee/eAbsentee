About HB238

Let your legislator know you support HB238! Steps:

  1. Find your legislator. For “Address,” use the address where you are registered to vote. After legislator names are shown, find your state delegate.
  2. Click “Email” under their name, then return to this page.
  3. Use below information to write email to your legislator.
    Subject: “Support HB 238: Extended deadline for absentee ballot return”
    Suggested Text:
    Dear Delegate ____,
    I am ____, one of your constituents. I encourage you to support HB 238, a bill sponsored by Del. Mark Sickles to ensure absentee ballots postmarked by Election Day are included in vote tallies.
    Absentee-by-mail voting allows every Virginian to participate in the democratic process, even if they are unable to vote in-person on Election Day. However, mail ballots take time to arrive at the offices of local registrars. As a result, the votes of some Virginians are lost because they arrived at the registrar’s office after 5:00 PM on Election Day.
    In particularly close elections, a few absentee ballots can flip the final result. Please support HB 238, as it would ensure that the election result accurately reflects the will of all voters who wished to express their opinion.
    BCC Button: (optional - please consider bcc’ing me on your email. I’d love to hear your message!) . robert@eAbsentee.org
