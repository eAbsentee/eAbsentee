About HB220

Let your legislator know you support HB220! Steps:

  1. Find your legislator. For “Address,” use the address where you are registered to vote. After legislator names are shown, find your state delegate.
  2. Click “Email” under their name, then return to this page.
  3. Use below information to write email to your legislator.
    Subject: “Support HB220: Prepaid postage for absentee ballots”
    Suggested Text:
    Dear Delegate ____,
    I am ____, one of your constituents. I encourage you to support HB 220, a bill sponsored by Del. Paul Krizek to offer prepaid postage on absentee ballots.
    Many people like me want to vote absentee in local, state, or national elections. But postage can be a barrier to voting, as it takes time to get to the post office and money to buy stamps. Additionally, many people who wish to vote lack the transportation necessary to get to either the polls or the post office.
    Please help eliminate this barrier to voting and make it easier for Virginians to vote.

    BCC Button: (optional - please consider bcc’ing me on your email. I’d love to hear your message!) . robert@eAbsentee.org
