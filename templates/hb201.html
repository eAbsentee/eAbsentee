About HB201

Let your legislator know you support HB201! Steps:

  1. Find your legislator. For “Address,” use the address where you are registered to vote. After legislator names are shown, find your state delegate.
  2. Click “Email” under their name, then return to this page.
  3. Use below information to write email to your legislator.
    Subject Button: “Support HB 201: Same-day registration”
    Text Button:
    Dear Delegate ____,
    I am ____, one of your constituents. I encourage you to support HB 201, a bill sponsored by Del. Hala Ayala to allow voters to register to vote on the same day they submit their ballot. Virginians lead busy lives, and often are too preoccupied to be aware of voter registration deadlines, which are typically over three weeks in advance of the respective election. These strict deadlines add an unnecessary step to voting, which makes it harder for Virginians to have their voices heard at the ballot box. As a passionate supporter of democracy, I know that this bill would make it easier for me and my neighbors to vote. Please help eliminate this barrier to voting support HB 201.
    BCC Button: (optional - please consider bcc’ing me on your email. I’d love to hear your message!) . robert@eAbsentee.org
