Absentee Ballot Information

If you need to follow up with your local registrar regarding your absentee ballot application, contact information can be found here.

Key upcoming deadlines. Absentee ballots can be returned in-person or by mail:

Absentee voters have until November 2 to visit their local voter registration office and vote in-person absentee. The local offices will be open Saturday, October 26, and Saturday, November 2, for individuals wishing to cast their absentee ballots in person.

Voters also may visit their local voter registration office during regular business hours through November 2 to vote absentee in-person.

All absentee ballots - including those mailed back - must be received by 7 p.m. on Election Day, November 5, in order to be counted
