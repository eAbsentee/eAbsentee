Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eAbsentee Ballot System?
The eAbsentee Ballot System allows voters to register for an absentee ballot online from their homes. Once you fill out the form, your information is securely and safely sent to the county registrar where it will be processed. They will send you an absentee ballot which you can fill out to cast your vote.
