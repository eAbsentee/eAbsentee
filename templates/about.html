About Us

This website is sponsored by Vote Absentee Virginia, a not-for-profit 501(c)4 organization based in Falls Church VA. It was formed to help broaden voter access across all parties in Virginia. It does not generate revenue from this site; its only source of revenue are civic-minded donors who believe that mailed-out ballots help increase voter participation in our democracy.
